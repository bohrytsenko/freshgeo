Our mission

At FreshGeo, our mission is to provide secure, private and uncensored Internet access to anyone, anywhere. To do so, we are pushing boundaries and developing the next generation of cutting-edge cybersecurity products and solutions.

Coming From Experience

In 2013, FreshGeo was an idea on a drawing board, today we’re a leading VPN service, trusted by over 1 million consumers and businesses worldwide. Building on decades of development and UX/UI experience, our knowledgeable team brings a variety of expertise to the development of a truly innovative SaaS cybersecurity company.

Amit Bareket, Co-Founder and CEO

Amit is a cyber expert and the author of 7 patents issued by the USPTO for storage, mobile applications and user interface. Prior to SaferVPN, Amit worked as a software engineer for major enterprises including IBM XIV Storage and BigBand Networks. He graduated Cum Laude with a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Economics from Tel Aviv University and served in the IDF's elite tech unit.

Sagi Gidali, Co-Founder and CPO

Sagi is a UX/UI leader who believes in keeping things simple, both in life and in tech. Prior to co-founding SaferVPN, Sagi worked for Siemens as a UX consultant, designing web applications that were used by major clients in the automotive industry, including GM and Volvo. He has a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Economics from Tel Aviv University and served in the IDF's elite tech unit.
